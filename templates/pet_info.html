Meet BANE (THE DC COMIC PUPPIES)

THE DC COMIC PUPPIES!!!

MEET BANE!!!

His Mama Katie showed up on someone's porch and refused to leave.
She was pregnant and covered in over 1,000 ticks and her gums were white!!

Katie gave birth to NINE puppies!!! Bane is one of her puppies!! All of the puppies were adopted but Bane was returned because his adopter worked too many long hours and thought he deserved better.

Bane is a very sweet boy with a great temperament!! he loves to play with other dogs and toys!!

Bane is about 6 months old. He has beautiful tri-colored markings!!! Want to meet this sweet boy?

Bane would do best in a home with another dog.
No apartments. This dog needs a safe, fenced yard to play in.
