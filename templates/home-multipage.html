ABOUT US

Your small contribution make a huge difference and encourage us to continue our works of success.

Our organization was founded by "animal lover" UTA students.

We believe that there should be no animal left unloved.

We endeavor to raise every possible "stray animals" and provide them the care they deserve.

We have a collective dream to find a proper home for every animals we have with us.

Read More

OUR MISSION

We thrive to connect animal lovers and all the lovely animals. This process will ensure the well-being of all the helpless animals and at same time helps make a never ending bonding.

OUR VISION

We dream of helping all those helpless animals by making food,shelter,proper hyegine etc. accessible with options for donation which will be used for them. We want to make a global impact to improve the lives of animals.

Made with Love

We created with website using flask, python, Bootstrap, and various other technoloies to get the ultimate version of animal shelter website.
